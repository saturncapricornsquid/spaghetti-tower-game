Marshmallow Challenge – Instructions

You have 10 minutes to build the tallest free‑standing structure.

• Use up to 20 spaghetti sticks
• Use as much or as little tape (glue) and string as you wish
• You may break spaghetti and reposition pieces
• The entire marshmallow must be on top
• The structure must stand without support

When the timer ends, hands off. Any structure being touched or supported is disqualified.

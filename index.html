Spaghetti Tower Challenge Team: —
Build the tallest free-standing tower. Place the top spaghetti piece highest before time ends.
Tip: Use Glue ON to connect pieces (tape/string). Rotate selected stick with Q/E or mouse wheel.
Top piece = the pink circle in the game.
⏱ 10:00
Controls

Click empty space to place spaghetti sticks (max 20). Drag to move. Rotate with Q/E or mouse wheel.
Rules — 10 minutes. Use up to 20 spaghetti sticks. Glue with tape/string. The top spaghetti piece must be the highest point and the structure must stand without support. When the timer ends: hands off.
